About InterviewMate

InterviewMate is a cutting-edge virtual mock interview platform designed to help job seekers prepare for professional interviews. Our tool leverages advanced technologies to provide personalized feedback on resume analysis, emotional cues, and communication skills.

This is a mock interview platform built with the collaboration of Y B Sai Prasad, R Bharath, P Varun Kumar, aimed at improving interview techniques and boosting confidence through simulated interview experiences. As one of our users says, "InterviewMate helps you feel more prepared and confident before walking into an actual job interview."

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world job applications by offering a simulated interview experience that improves confidence and interview techniques.
