How It Works

Pairup

We will match you with a suitable peer based on your preferences. The interview will be 1 Hr 30 Mins.

Interview Your Peer

For the first half (45 Mins), you interview your peer based on the question and answer we provide (or vice versa).

Peer Interviews You

For the second half (45 Mins), your peer interviews you based on the question and answer we provide (or vice versa).

Evaluate Each Other

After completion, you and your peer provide feedback. Work on the areas you lack and repeat until you are confident.
